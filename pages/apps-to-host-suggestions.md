# Apps to host - [suggestions]{style="color: green;"}

* 🍅 Grocy to manage your stock
* 🍿 Jellyfin to host your movies and series
* 🔊 Audiobookshelf to host your podcasts
* 🔑 Vaultwarden, bitwarden to manage your passwords
* 🐣 Babybuddy to keep track of baby activity
* 📝 Trilium to keep your notes
* 💸 Fireflyiii to manage your finances
* 📚 Calibre-web to manage your e-books
* ✅ Vikunja to manage your todo lists
* 📄 Paperless-ng to digitise and store your documents

For more ideas see [Awesome
Selfhosted](https://github.com/awesome-selfhosted/awesome-selfhosted) or
[selfh.st](https://selfh.st/)
